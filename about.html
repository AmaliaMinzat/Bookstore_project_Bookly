About Us

Our story

The story of how our bookstore first started dates from a long time ago, in 1970 it was first opened by a Literature teacher William Mellow, after that the bookstore stayed in the familly and as for today it still is in the Mellow familly, our great owner being his great granddaughter, Melissa Hills.

Our bookshop today

Today we are still open but things might have changed from what you espect from an old bookstore, we still keep some old things to remind us where we started, and we are forever grateful for what it has become today.
